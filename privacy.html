3. How We Use Information

All data processing occurs entirely on your device. We use locally stored information solely for:

  • Performing INR calculations using the WHO-standard formula
  • Maintaining a personal log of your calculation history
  • Displaying educational content about anticoagulation therapy
  • Providing reference ranges and safety information

No data is ever transmitted to our servers, third parties, or external services.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Zero Data Sharing Policy: We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any information with third parties. The app does not contain advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or data brokers.

We will never:

  • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share information with insurance companies or employers
  • Transmit data to cloud services or external servers
  • Use your information for targeted advertising
  • Provide access to government agencies (no data is available to share)

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security depends on your device's built-in protections:

  • Data is stored using Android's application sandbox
  • Access is restricted to the INR Calculator app only
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • No encryption is applied (data never leaves your device)

Your responsibility: Protect your device with a screen lock, and be cautious when using shared devices, as calculation logs are accessible to anyone with physical access to your unlocked device.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

6.1 Retention

Calculation logs are retained indefinitely on your device until you manually delete them or uninstall the application.

6.2 How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Individual entries: Open the Log page → tap the trash icon next to any entry → confirm deletion
  • All logs: Open the Log page → tap "Clear All Logs" → confirm
  • Complete removal: Uninstall INR Calculator from your device (deletes all associated data)
